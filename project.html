Golden Retriever Chamber Ensemble

With the help of a grant from the Regional Arts and Culture Council, Golden Retriever put together an ensemble of top-notch musicians from Portland's classical, improvisation and electronic music scenes to debut a new piece alongside them at The Old Church in downtown Portland. For this project I was asked to design the poster and showcard for the event.
